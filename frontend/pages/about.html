About

UniDorms is a modern dorm management platform designed to simplify everyday student living. From room assignments to meals and maintenance requests, we bring convenience and organization to campus life.

Our Story

Connecting Dorms, Students, and Staff — Seamlessly

UniDorms was created to make dorm life smarter and stress-free. What began as a small student project grew into a full-featured system helping dorms stay organized and connected.

2022

The idea for UniDorms was born — focused on making student housing more transparent and efficient.

2023

The first prototype launched, allowing admins to manage rooms and requests online.

2024

Meal planning and announcement features were added to connect dorm residents even more.

2025

UniDorms became a complete dorm management system, trusted by students and staff to handle every part of dorm life.

Our Mission

To simplify and enhance dorm life through an intuitive platform that connects students and administrators for efficient communication and management.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital solution for student dormitories, fostering comfort, connection, and community across every campus.
